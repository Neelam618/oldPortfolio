About

I have also worked as a part-time freelancer to build websites for local businesses and helped them establish their online presence.

I am thrilled to keep advancing on my learning journey and offer my skills and expertise to new projects. At present, I am actively seeking a full-time role as a front-end developer to leverage my passion and abilities in creating innovative solutions. I am eager to join a dynamic team where I can collaborate, learn from my peers, and contribute to the development of top-notch products.
